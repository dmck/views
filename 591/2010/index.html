CHAPTER 440 (2010)
SECTION 591

440.591 Administrative procedure; rulemaking authority.

The department, the Financial Services Commission, the agency, and the Department of Education may adopt rules pursuant to ss. 120.536(1) and 120.54 to implement the provisions of this chapter conferring duties upon them.

s. 46, ch. 90-201; s. 44, ch. 91-1; s. 115, ch. 98-200; s. 55, ch. 2002-194; s. 495, ch. 2003-261; s. 76, ch. 2005-2.
